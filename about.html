ABOUT
Shib.Ai, short for the 'Shiba' dog breed and the world 'AI', is an interactive pet companion bot powered by our latest AI model - PetGPT 3.0. This pet model was developed in accordance with our vision, which is to develop a virtual platform to inspire and offer companionship to people, particularly those who are now experiencing mental health issues throughout Malaysia. Through this initiative, we aim to spread awareness about mental health to help people find hope and optimism.
